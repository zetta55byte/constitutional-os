## The Standard

Constitutional OS is the governance substrate for AI agents.
The four membranes (safety, reversibility, pluralism, human primacy)
correspond to the four failure modes of agentic systems.
The three integration hooks (propose_plan, propose_action, propose_delta)
provide complete governance coverage at intent, capability, and state-transition layers.

**You govern deltas, not thoughts. That's why it scales.**
